Comprehensive eDiary Features

Medication Tracking

Enable patients to easily log their medication intake, set reminders, and track adherence throughout the trial period.

Real-time Data Reporting

Access up-to-date patient data and generate comprehensive reports for efficient trial management and quick decision-making.
